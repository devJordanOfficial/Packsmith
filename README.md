# Packsmith

> Build, manage, and share Minecraft modpacks — from anywhere.

Packsmith is a free, open web application for assembling Minecraft modpacks. Browse mods directly from CurseForge, organize them into named packs, and export a launcher-ready `.zip` — all from your browser, all synced to your account.

---

## Features

- **Browse CurseForge** — search and filter mods, resource packs, and shader packs without leaving the app
- **Build your pack** — assemble mods into named modpacks with configurable Minecraft version, mod loader, and version
- **Cross-device sync** — your packs live in the cloud, accessible from any device
- **One-click export** — generate a CurseForge-compatible `.zip` ready to import into any CurseForge launcher
- **Free, forever** — no ads, no premium tiers, no nonsense

---

## Roadmap

**Phase 1 — Modpack Management**
Authentication, modpack creation and editing, and a personal dashboard to manage all your packs.

**Phase 2 — CurseForge Integration**
Browse and search mods directly from CurseForge, add them to your packs, and pin specific versions.

**Phase 3 — Cross-Device Sync**
Everything tied to your account and accessible from any device with a browser.

**Phase 4 — Export**
Generate a valid CurseForge `manifest.json` and download your pack as a `.zip` ready to import.
